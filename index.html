About

I started in cinema, studied storytelling and image, and chose sound as my main path. Since then I have worked on fiction films, TV series, reality shows, documentaries and live events – covering both production sound and post-production audio.

My strengths are clear communication on set, calm problem-solving when something breaks, and a strong ear for detail in dialogue and atmospheres. I’m comfortable in multi-camera environments, using radio mics, recorders and basic networking to keep audio, video and comms aligned.
